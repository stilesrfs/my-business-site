Our Mission

Enabling Natural Gas to Be Part of the Clean Energy Future

E4 Energy Services exists at the intersection of practical engineering and environmental urgency. Natural gas compression is essential infrastructure — but it's also responsible for a disproportionate share of methane and carbon emissions from the oil & gas sector.

We build the technology that makes compression cleaner. Not by replacing compression with something else — but by solving the specific engineering problems that cause compression to emit what it emits. Rod packing that vents methane. Piston seals that require oil and leak. Exhaust stacks that vent CO₂ when that CO₂ could be captured and sold.

Our founders have deep roots in the compression industry. The technology we build is informed by decades of understanding what actually happens at a compressor station — and what operators actually need to make emissions reduction work in the field.
